üöÄ About the Project
In a scenario of seeking financial autonomy and planning, investing in Real Estate Investment Funds (FIIs) emerges as an excellent alternative for building passive income. However, simulating and planning the future of these investments, considering different market scenarios and personal goals, can be complex and unintuitive.

The FIIs ARCHITECT is an interactive tool developed in Microsoft Excel that transcends the simple projection of "how much I'll have." It allows the user to architect their FII investment plan, optimizing contributions and visualizing the growth of capital and passive income under various market conditions, focusing on achieving clear financial goals.

This project was created as part of the "Create a Winning Portfolio" challenge from the "Excel with AI" course by DIO, through Santander Open Academy.

‚ú® Key Features
Passive Income Goal Simulator: Discover the estimated monthly contribution needed to reach your desired passive income goal within a specific timeframe, through interactive optimization.

Dynamic Scenario Analysis: Explore the impact of different market scenarios (Optimistic, Base, and Pessimistic) on the yield and appreciation of Papel FIIs (debt-based) and Tijolo FIIs (equity-based).

Strategic Diversification: Simulate the allocation of contributions between Papel FIIs and Tijolo FIIs, observing how different portfolio compositions affect your results.

Detailed Month-by-Month Projection: Track the evolution of your capital and passive income throughout the entire simulation period, with dynamic calculations that adjust to your plan.

Intuitive KPIs (Key Performance Indicators): Visualize the most important results (final accumulated capital, last month's income, total invested) clearly summarized in the Dashboard.

Dynamic Line Charts: Visually monitor the growth of total capital and monthly passive income, with charts that automatically adapt to the selected scenarios and simulation years.

Intelligent Insights: Receive automated suggestions and contextual analyses generated by the spreadsheet, guiding your decisions and offering an "AI-driven" perspective on your financial future.

User-Friendly and Professional Interface: A clean, intuitive Dashboard with a focus on user experience (UX), designed to facilitate interaction and data comprehension.

Robust Data Validation: Ensures the integrity of user input, alerting and blocking invalid values, such as the allocation percentages needing to sum up to 100%.
